Captain America VS Iron Man

Iron Man/Tony Stark is a guy who gets kidnapped by terrorists. after being there for a while being forced to build weapons for them he turns on them and makes his very first suit of armor. Which eventually falls apart while he escapes, he was soon found by the US Military and he is brought back to America. He goes onto build many more suits of armor which he uses to fight evil people.

Captain America/Steve Rogers is a guy who is a scrawny type of guy who wants to fight in the Military. But he can't because he wasn't physically capable. After a while since he joined a scientist comes up to him and talks to hime about the super soldier serum. After he gets the serum he becomes enhanced in his speed and strength. He goes onto fight in the 1945 war before making a great sacrifice while fighting the main villan and gets frozen in ice only to wake up 70 years later.

After being apart of the Avengers team. Both Iron Man and Captain America disagree about the Sovia Accords, Captain America is all for signing them while Iron Man is not. They both have an argument before fighting it out. But first they both get a team of poeple to fight with them.
